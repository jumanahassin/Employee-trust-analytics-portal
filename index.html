💡 Recommendation Insights

🔍 Strategic Interventions Based on ITS Analysis

1

Communication scored lowest at 46.8% — implement structured daily stand-ups, feedback loops, and cross-departmental communication workshops to improve transparency.

2

ITS6 (task reminders) had the lowest item score (37.6%) — introduce autonomous task management systems to reduce micromanagement perceptions.

3

Only ITS23 (helping underperformers) crossed the High Trust threshold (67%) — leverage this strength through peer mentoring and buddy programs.

4

Managerial Competency at 53.5% suggests leadership development programs, 360-degree feedback, and coaching sessions for team leads and managers.

5

ML model (RF accuracy 91.3%) identifies 18 high-risk employees — proactive one-on-one check-ins and customised engagement plans are recommended immediately.
